About Memorio

"Memorio is a simple memory game. The aim of the game is to find 6 pairs in 20 seconds. For each matching pair, you get 1 point.

The game is developed in Visual Studio using Apache Cordova"

Author's Word

“Hello!

This is my first mobile app! Maybe it isn't the best one you have seen, but it is special to me. I made Memorio in 7 days with no prior experience in developing mobile apps with just a pinch of help from my teacher Benjamin Talic, and I think it turned out pretty good.

Actually I am not a developer, I am an economist - or rather I was. Five months ago I decided to change that and joined BitCamp, an intensive programming bootcamp. Now I am here, with my first published app and I could not be prouder."
